Lemons to Lemonade

Not Just Refreshing

Lemonade is not only a refreshing drink, but it can also have a positive impact on the environment. At our shop, we take steps to reduce our carbon footprint and support sustainable practices whenever possible.

All Local

We source our lemons from local farmers who use eco-friendly farming methods, such as organic farming and integrated pest management. By supporting local agriculture, we help reduce transportation emissions and promote biodiversity in our region.

Biodegradable

We also strive to reduce waste by using reusable cups and straws, and by composting our lemon peels and other organic waste. Our cups and straws are made from biodegradable materials, and we encourage our customers to bring their own reusable containers whenever possible.

So when you drink our lemonade, not only will you quench your thirst with a delicious and refreshing drink

but you'll also be supporting a sustainable and environmentally conscious business.

Together, we can make a difference and create a better world for ourselves and future generations.

When did the
Juicing Began?

Lemonado Shop was started by two childhood friends who shared a passion for creating new and exciting drinks. They noticed a lack of high-quality lemonade options in their neighborhood and decided to take matters into their own hands.

How Did It Become More Than A Lemonade

Over the years, Lemonado Shop has expanded its offerings to include a variety of different flavors and sizes, all made with the same care and attention to detail that our founders put into their original recipe. Despite our growth, we remain committed to our roots as a small, community-focused business, and we take pride in providing our customers with the highest quality drinks and service.

So whether you're looking for a classic lemonade or something a little more adventurous, come on down to Lemonado Shop and taste the difference for yourself!
